How to cook pasta

in easy 5 steps

1) Prepare all the ingredients

  • spaghetti
  • tomatoes
  • basil
  • salt
  • black pepper
  • olive oil

You also will need..

  1. a large pot
  2. a pan

2) Bring the water in the pot to boil

when the water is about to boil, add a punch of rock salt

3) Prepare the tomatoes sauce

  1. chop the tomatoes into small pieces
  2. heat a pan putting in it one or two spoons of olive oil and a clove of garlic
  3. remove the garlic and add the tomatoes, a little of salt and pepper
  4. cook slowly for fifteen minutes

4) Cook pasta

when the water boils, add a punch of rock salt and putt the spaghetti in it.
Cook slowly without lid for the time indicated on the packaging, usually betwen 8 and 10 minutes.

Tip

The perfect pasta is always "al dente", which means a little bit undercooked. That is beacause you will spend the last two minutes cooking pasta in the pan with its tomatoes.

5) Complete your dish

  1. When the pasta is "al dente", drain the water with a strainer
  2. add the pasta to the tomatoes sauce , turn on the heat and mix for two minutes
  3. turn off the heat and put the pasta in a soup dish
  4. drizzle olive oil, add parmesan end enjoy your pasta!
